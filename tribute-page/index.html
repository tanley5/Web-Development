The Great Conqueror From Macedonia

A Mosaic of Alexander the Great

Accomplishments:

  • King of Macedonia (336-323 BC)
  • Hegemon of Hellenic League, Strategos Autokrator of Greece against Achaemenid Empire (336 BC)
  • Pharaoh of Egypt (332-323 BC)
  • King of Persia (330-323 BC)
  • Lord of Asia (331-323 BC)

Biography:

  • Born: 20 or 21 July 356 BC
  • Died: 10 or 11 June 323 BC
  • Spouse:
    • Roxana of Bactria
    • Stateira II of Persia
    • Parysatis II of Persia
  • Full Name: Alexander III of Macedon

Why I like Him

I like Alexander the Great because of his drive. I can't begin to imagine being tutored by Aristoteles while growing up. Furthermore, he pushed to conquer continuously. Even though that might have been the cause of his death, I can't hellp but admire him.
